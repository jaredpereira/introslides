Constructivism

A theory of knowledge

Knowledge is constructed in an individual based on the interaction between their experiences and ideas

Stemming from Jean Piaget

Drawing from a genetic epistemology perspective

Focused on assimilation and accomodation

Constructionism

A learning theory

Weakly: Learning by doing

Strongly: Learning by creating meaningful artifcats

Championed by Seymour Papert

Recasts Piaget as an epistemologist not pediatric psychologist

The materials with which knowledge is constructed are sourced from one's environment
